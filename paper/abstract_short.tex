We present a parameter-free, topology-labeled loop-class library
for cross-sector observables, mapping each registered
Standard-Model and cosmological observable to a deterministic
$(n, g, s)$ tuple over the topology classes
(Yukawa-damping, PMNS self-energy, Pure-self-energy,
Resummed-propagator, Generation, Sub-generation, EW-mixed,
Cosmological-density, Pure-sync). The library factors
$(1 \pm \gamma/4, 1\pm \gamma/(2N_{\rm gen}), \ldots)$ are
multiplicative dressings of an underlying System-$\mathcal R$
algebraic identity for each observable. A 29-row closure table
audits the library against PDG/FLAG/HFLAV/NuFIT/Planck anchors:
under the strict EXACT cut $|r|<0.4\%$ we find EXACT-under-$\mathcal R$
closures for $V_{ub}$ ($=\gamma/N_{\rm gen}^{3}=1/270$, $0.37\%$),
$V_{ts}$ ($=\alpha_{\xi}/(2(2d+N_{\rm gen}))=9/220$, $0.22\%$),
$m_{b}/m_{\tau}$ ($=2+\gamma+s_{\rm face}=47/20$, $0.11\%$), and
others; PRECISE-tier closures for $\Sigma m_{\nu}=0.0593$\,eV
(canonical refined $m_{1}=\gamma^{2}m_{3}$ closure, validated
against DESI DR2 BAO $<0.0642$\,eV under $\Lambda$CDM;
see P4B Sec.~``Neutrino-mass-sum version history'' for the
supersession trail), $V_{cs}$, $V_{tb}$, and the Bekenstein--Hawking
$1/4$ identity. The spectral Yukawa-eigenvalue pipeline supplies
the algorithmic mass-origin layer with explicit L/R chirality
splitting on a five-factor mass operator; it returns the
12-parameter fermion-sector closure (top mass $174.1$\,GeV at
EXACT $0.7\%$, CKM bi-unitary $9/9$ at FACTOR2-or-better, Jarlskog
$J_{\rm CP}=2.28\!\times\!10^{-5}$ at FACTOR2) with zero fitted
parameters. The same $\varepsilon_{\rm sync}^{2}=\gamma/2$ identity
that closes the cosmological dark-sector observables also supplies
the ninth layer of the cosmological-constant dressing (P4B). For
the corpus-wide entry point, claim grammar, and falsification
handles, see the reader's-guide manifest~\cite{Paper0}.
